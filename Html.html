Grey Wolf

(Canis lupus)


*This image is owned by Oregonlive

Since the Grey wolf is highley specialized in hunting, it has superior stamina and can stalk and chase prey for days without stopping.

Eyes:
The grey wolf's eyes give them the ability to see in the night.
Socialization:
Wolfs are a social animal, they hunt in packs. they also have an "Alpha"
Alpha:
Alpha's are chosen through a fight of the strongest wolf. The alpha is the leader of the pack. In turn they are the first to lead an attack.
Communication:
Wolves mainly communicate through howls. They have different pitches for different types of information.

Even though its called the Grey Wolf, most of the time their fur color isn't grey but more of a light brown. Usually have yellowish brown eyes, pointy ears, a long narrow snout, and razor sharp teeth.
